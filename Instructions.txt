Instructions:
1 - Launch/Run code (PREFERABLY ON WINDOWS - PyGame will not run on my Mac, but runs fine on my Windows).
2 - Make sure colors.csv is downloaded in relative path.
3 - Choose your method of color input (RGB or Color Name).
4 - Input a color.
5 - Input the length of the color palette (1-6).
6 - You can now read and view the output palette in the Pygame window. 
7 - Tap the ‘X’ or anywhere on the pygame window to close.
8 - After you close PyGame, choose if you want to continue or not back in IDLE (‘Yes’ or ‘No’).
